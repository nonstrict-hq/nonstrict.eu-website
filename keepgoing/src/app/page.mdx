## No numbers, focus on the workout {{ date: '2024-11-01T00:00Z' }}

Choose an activity, set a goal and Keep Going show your progress by filling up the screen as you go. Helping you see your progress at a glance, no more peeking at tiny numbers.

You can also choose to set a target heart rate zone and pace. If you go outside your target zone a clear arrow on screen, haptic and audio feedback will guide you back to the right pace.

### <SparkleIcon /> Highlights

- Supports walking, running, rolling, biking, handbiking, swimming and rowing
- Set a distance, duration or calorie burn goal for your workout
- Optionally set a target intensity based on heart rate zone or pace
- Get automatic and on demand audio feedback on your goal and intensity target
- Mirrors every workout type to your iPhone
